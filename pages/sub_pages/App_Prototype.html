App Prototype

Me and a 4 other students designed an app prototype and came up with the idea for an app. The finished result was above my expectations but I guess that's how it is when you do it right.

Here is the link to the prototype. Me and 2 others designed the app while other 2 did user testing to see if there were any problems with the app.

Our main UC campus has scooters that students can use and we decided to make the availability (ex: how charged it is, where the scooters are located) of scooters shown on an app. It also shows where nearby scooters are.
