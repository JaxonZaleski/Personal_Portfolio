Battleship

As I grew more accustomed to coding with Graphical User Interfaces (GUI) I had to move one level up in difficulty by making a single player battleship game that keeps score by seeing how many misses you got in a row, with a maximum of 3, and interrupts your gameplay to tell you that you lost.
